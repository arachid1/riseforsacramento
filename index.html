About Us

R.I.S.E. for Sacramento mobilizes the Sacramento region around our hospital workers and locally-owned restaurants to sustain their livelihoods and protect the health of those negatively impacted by COVID-19.

Our Mission

We provide nutritious meals from locally-owned restaurants and protective equipment from residents to our hospital workers. We want to ensure that every hospital worker is protected against this virus and is properly nourished throughout the day, while also uplifting businesses and protecting the jobs of locally-owned restaurants.

Relief

Our mission is to bring RELIEF to our front-liners who are fighting this invisible war against COVID-19.

Inspire

We INSPIRE others to stay home...

Support

...and SUPPORT troubled small businesses.

Entrust

We ENTRUST our volunteers to safely deliver freshly cooked nutritious, fun meals from local family owned restaurants to feed and boost morale to the hard working and exhausted healthcare providers as well as collect N95 masks.
